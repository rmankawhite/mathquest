⭐ MATHQUEST ⭐

Welcome!

MathQuest is a math adventure game where you explore a maze, make animal friends, and earn gold coins by solving math problems. Collect enough coins to get past Shasta the Dragon and move on to the next level!

How to Move

Use the arrow keys or WASD on your keyboard to move your hero around the maze. The maze starts dark — explore to light it up and discover what's hiding nearby.

Making Friends and Earning Coins

When you get close to an animal friend, bump into them to start a math challenge. Answer correctly and you earn a gold coin. Each level has 11 friends to find, but you only need a certain number of coins to pass Shasta the Dragon and complete the level.

If you get a math problem wrong, don't worry — you'll get a new one to try right away.

The Levels

There are 5 levels, and each one gets harder:

  • Level 1 — addition and subtraction with numbers 11–50. Collect 5 coins to pass.
  • Level 2 — numbers 51–99. Collect 6 coins to pass.
  • Level 3 — numbers 101–333. Collect 7 coins to pass.
  • Level 4 — numbers 101–667. Collect 8 coins to pass.
  • Level 5 — numbers 101–999. Collect 10 coins to pass.

Keywords and the Secret Passcode

Every time you complete a level, you earn a secret keyword. Write it down! You can enter your keyword on the start screen next time you play to skip ahead to where you left off.

Complete all five levels and the keywords spell out a secret passcode. This passcode will change weekly.

Choosing Your Hero

Pick from four heroes at the start — Dog, Tiger, Monkey, or Capybara. They all play the same way, so choose your favorite!

Tips

  • Explore the whole map — friends can be hiding anywhere in the maze.
  • You don't have to find all 11 friends, just enough to earn the coins you need. But the more you find, the more practice you get!
  • Check the top of the screen to track your coins and how many friends you've found.
  • Use the ♪ ON / OFF button in the top corner to toggle the music.

Happy exploring — good luck, Math Hero!
